Overview
Microsoft Health and Life Sciences division is looking for an iOS engineer who is passionate about our mission to empower medical professionals to deliver patient care without the long hours of patient documentation. There has never been a more exciting time to be working in healthcare at Microsoft. Our Health & Life Sciences Solutions (HLS) organization is an interdisciplinary team of product managers, designers, engineers, and clinicians who are designing, developing and deploying next-generation healthcare solutions powered by the Microsoft Cloud for healthcare organizations around the world.

 

We are looking to hire a Senior Software Engineer to join our team. As a Senior Software Engineer, you will be an integral member of a mobile development team within HLS Solutions that is working to build mobile apps and cloud software to empower clinicians to achieve more with groundbreaking healthcare-oriented copilots. The candidate will be excited about building apps that capture clinician-patient conversations and make complex AI analysis and generation operations simple and intuitive for the provider. Through collaboration with UX designers and teams of cloud and web developers, you will bring the power of generative AI to mobile devices and develop and deploy new technologies and experiences centered around driving positive healthcare outcomes.

 

Microsoft’s mission is to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more. As employees we come together with a growth mindset, innovate to empower others, and collaborate to realize our shared goals. Each day we build on our values of respect, integrity, and accountability to create a culture of inclusion where everyone can thrive at work and beyond.
